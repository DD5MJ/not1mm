Status:	Active
 	Geographic Focus:	Switzerland
 	Participation:	Worldwide
 	Mode:	CW, SSB, Digital

SWL

3 points per QSO with different continent

Each DXCC country once per band
 	Score Calculation:	Total score = total QSO points x total mults
